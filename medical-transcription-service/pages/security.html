Security & Compliance

Enterprise-grade protection for sensitive healthcare data. At MedTranscribe, security isn't a feature — it's our foundation.

HIPAA COMPLIANT SOC 2 TYPE II ISO 27001
Data Privacy

Your Trust, Our Responsibility

We understand the critical importance of maintaining patient confidentiality. Our entire platform is built from the ground up to exceed the stringent requirements of healthcare regulations.

  • ✔
    Business Associate Agreements (BAA)

    We sign BAAs with all our covered entity clients, assuming our role in the HIPAA compliance chain.

  • ✔
    Employee Vetting

    All transcriptionists undergo rigorous background checks and mandatory annual HIPAA training.

  • ✔
    No Data Selling

    We never sell, share, or monetize your patient data. Your information is used only to provide the service.

Technical Safeguards

Defense in Depth

Multi-layered security protocols to ensure data integrity and confidentiality at every stage.

🔒

Encryption Standards

Data is encrypted using military-grade AES-256 at rest in our databases and file storage. All transit occurs over TLS 1.3 connections.

🏢

Hardened Infrastructure

Hosted in Tier 3, SOC 2 Type II certified data centers within the United States. 24/7 physical security and biometric access controls.

🛡️

Network Security

Continuous vulnerability scanning, automated intrusion detection systems, and annual professional penetration testing.

Data Retention & Deletion

We maintain a strict "Privacy by Design" approach to data retention:

Audio Files

Audio recordings are automatically purged from our production systems 7 days after the final transcript is delivered and accepted.

Transcripts

Completed transcripts are stored in our secure portal for 30 days by default, after which they are permanently deleted unless otherwise specified by your account's retention policy.

Access Control

Granular controls ensure that only authorized personnel can access the specific information they need for their roles.

🆔
MFA Required
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all internal and client portal access.
🎭
Role-Based Access (RBAC)
Permission-level controls for clinicians, administrators, and transcriptionists.
📜
Audit Logging
Immutable logs record every access, export, and modification of patient data.

Security FAQ

Common questions about how we handle and protect your clinic's data.

Is my data stored outside the United States? +

No. All MedTranscribe data, including backups and transient processing files, is stored exclusively on HIPAA-compliant servers located within the continental United States.

Do you use AI models that 'learn' from my data? +

While we use AI to assist our transcriptionists, we use private, siloed models. We DO NOT use your data to train public models or any system that could potentially leak information across client boundaries.

How do I get a copy of your SOC 2 report? +

Our current SOC 2 Type II report is available to prospective and current enterprise clients upon request and execution of an NDA. Please contact security@medtranscribe.com.
