About MedTranscribe

Founded by healthcare professionals who experienced the documentation burden firsthand — and built a better solution.

Our Mission

Freeing Physicians from Documentation Burden

Physicians spend an average of 2 hours on documentation for every hour of patient care. MedTranscribe was built to change that — delivering accurate, HIPAA-compliant transcriptions that let healthcare professionals focus on what they do best: caring for patients.

Founded in 2018 by Dr. James Chen and healthcare technology veteran Lisa Monroe, MedTranscribe has grown from a 10-person startup to a trusted partner for 500+ providers across 42 states.
